# Finalisasi taksonomi penyesatan biaya (P1–P6, R1)

Status: ready-for-human
Difficulty: hard

## Problem

The taxonomy in `project_brief.md` §8.1 + Lampiran C is a proposal. Before the injection pipeline (Fase 2) can be built, each category needs: (1) a crisp inclusion/exclusion rule, (2) at least 2 real-world example offers per category, (3) a decision on whether P5 (visual framing) can co-occur labels are assigned at text level or only at render level.

The label set defined here is the contract for the entire dataset — changing it later means relabeling everything (brief §17: "Titik paling menentukan ada di Fase 1 dan 2").

## Why human

Category boundaries encode domain judgment about Indonesian lending practice (what counts as "menyesatkan" vs merely terse) and carry legal-framing risk (brief §16.4). `researcher` can prepare a decision document with options, but the final call is the user's.

## Acceptance

- `CONTEXT.md` taxonomy table updated with inclusion/exclusion rules per category.
- ADR recording the finalized taxonomy (Status: Accepted).
- Follow-up `medium` issues filed for the injection transform of each finalized category.
